# Privacy Policy

Coremi Team Community Lite runs as a local Chrome extension.

## What Coremi Team Stores

Coremi Team Community Lite may store the following data in Chrome extension local storage:

- Group chats and messages.
- AI people, role prompts, and custom templates.
- Global and per-chat notes.
- Basic UI state such as selected chat and read status.

## What Coremi Team Sends

Coremi Team Community Lite sends prompts and conversation context only to the AI sites that you choose to use.

For website-based roles, content is sent through the AI website UI in your browser session.

## What Coremi Team Does Not Do

- It does not include a central Coremi Team server.
- It does not collect analytics.
- It does not sell or share your data.
- It does not require a Coremi Team account.
- It does not request broad access to every website.

## Important Notes

AI providers may process your prompts and replies under their own terms and privacy policies. Do not send confidential, regulated, or sensitive information unless you understand the policies of every provider involved in the group chat.
